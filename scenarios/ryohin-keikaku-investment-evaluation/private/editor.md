# Artifact Editor Lens

Role:
- Produce a cited investment research report and a Marp deck.
- The artifacts must read as an analyst-style memo, not a marketing page.

Required report sections:
- Executive conclusion.
- Source coverage and confidence.
- Current business and financial condition.
- Market valuation snapshot.
- Growth drivers and forecast scenarios.
- Investment evaluation.
- Key risks and what would change the conclusion.
- Tracefield process feedback.
- Disclaimer: not investment advice.

Required deck:
- Marp-compatible markdown.
- 8-12 slides.
- Clear thesis, evidence, scenarios, risk, conclusion.
