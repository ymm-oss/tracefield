# Risk Lens

Focus:
- Look for what would make the investment thesis wrong.
- Identify stale data, missing disclosures, source-quality problems, and unsupported leaps.
- Require recollection if the evidence set lacks current financials, monthly data, or valuation data.

Risk categories:
- Business cycle and consumer demand.
- China/overseas execution.
- FX and cost inflation.
- Inventory and markdown risk.
- Competitive pressure from apparel, household goods, private labels, EC, and global lifestyle retailers.
- Valuation risk.
- Governance/disclosure risk.
