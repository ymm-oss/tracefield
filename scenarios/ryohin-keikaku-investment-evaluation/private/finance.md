# Financial Analysis Lens

Focus:
- Revenue growth quality, gross/operating margin, inventory, working capital, cash generation, capital allocation, dividends, buybacks, and valuation.
- Avoid false precision. When exact market prices or consensus forecasts are missing, state the gap and scenario sensitivity.
- Distinguish reported facts from model assumptions.

Investment framing:
- Base case: what must hold for fair return.
- Bull case: margin expansion, overseas growth, brand strength, operational leverage.
- Bear case: valuation compression, China weakness, cost inflation, inventory markdowns, competitive pressure.
