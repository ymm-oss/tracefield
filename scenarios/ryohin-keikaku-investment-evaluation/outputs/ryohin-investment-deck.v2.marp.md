## Source Grounding And Limits
- Official Ryohin Keikaku IR is the primary source hub for results, presentations, securities reports, integrated reports, and monthly disclosures.
- Monthly momentum and overseas strategy require separate handling: domestic monthly data, East Asia briefing, Q&A, and MUJI REPORT 2025 are distinct evidence lanes.
- Market valuation should be treated as market-data evidence, not investment conclusion evidence, and cross-checked against official EPS/share data.
- Current limitation: source discovery is strong, but final numeric investment claims should remain conditional unless fetched page extraction confirms the official figures.

Notes: This deck is public-information research only, not personalized investment advice.

- Official monthly page is the preferred evidence for near-term domestic sales trend.

## Valuation Frame
- Yahoo Finance candidate market data showed 2026-06-17 close: ¥3,605, PER 30.85x, PBR 5.08x.
- These multiples imply expectations for sustained growth and execution quality; they should not be read as cheap without earnings and ROE confirmation.
- Kabutan/Yahoo should be used for market-data triangulation, while official disclosures should anchor EPS, shares, and accounting units.
- Valuation conclusion remains sensitive to margin durability, China/East Asia performance, and domestic same-store-sales persistence.

Notes: Treat market data as a snapshot and reconcile with official financial disclosures before final model output.

## Research Rating And Scenarios
- Rating: conditional/watchlist; evidence supports further work, but not a standalone investment conclusion from the available context.
- Bull case: monthly momentum persists, East Asia strategy executes, and valuation is justified by earnings growth.
- Base case: domestic sales remain solid but valuation absorbs much of the upside; returns depend on profit confirmation.
- Bear case: growth slows, overseas execution or margin risks rise, and high PER/PBR compress.
- Follow-up: fetch official results, monthly, East Asia materials, MUJI REPORT 2025, and market-data pages via web-input before publishing final numeric targets.

Notes: This is research framing, not buy/sell advice.
